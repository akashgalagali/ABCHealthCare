About Us

ABC Healthcare

ABC Healthcare is a Mumbai-based pharma company which sells medicines throughout India as per the demand it receives from multiple hospitals and clinics. It has been operating in India for the last 15 years. We make the best use of Mobile, App and Web Technology to make sure that you get access to the best health products, with attractive discounts in the shortest time possible. But remember, discounted medicines does not mean a discount in quality! Most often, the consumers are unaware of how they get their medicine and therefore, do not trust the e-pharmacy completely. PharmEasy works towards keeping all of their customers informed and provides a transparent platform so as to provide the best service possible.
